3. assignment operators :   =    assign                             a=b

                            /=   divide and assign                  a/=b

4. logical operators    :   &&   logical and                        a && b
                            ||   logical or                         a || b
                            !    logical not                        a ! b

5. bitwise operators    :   &    bitwise and                        a & b
